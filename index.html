Sambhal (formerly Budaun) Alumni Welfare Association of Navodaya (SAWAN)

Introduction

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV) is a system of central government-run residential schools in India, established under the National Policy on Education-1986. SAWAN is the registered association formed by alumni of JNV Sambhal/Budaun.

Mission

SAWAN builds lifelong relationships by engaging alumni, students, parents, and supporters of JNV Sambhal.

Vision

To foster an engaged alumni community supporting the success of JNV Sambhal.
